Welcome to check-your-ads.com! Our site is all about watching and discovering interesting and weird ads. Simply watch the 4 ad prompts and submit any cool or strange ads you come across on our Discord server at https://discord.gg/ddSY4R3U. Then, let the community vote on which ad they think is the weirdest. The ad with the most votes will win 50% of the ad revenue from the site, and we pick a winner every week. So, join us and start exploring the world of ads!"
